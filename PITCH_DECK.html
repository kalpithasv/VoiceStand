How VoiceStand Works

🔐 User logs in → Location captured (~1km radius)
↓
📸 Takes photo + writes complaint description
↓
🤖 AI validates: Does image match text?
↓
✅ Post created with validation badge
↓
🗳️ Community votes (5 hours to expiry)
↓
⏰ Auto-finalized: Upvotes=Valid, Downvotes=Wrong
